Where to Find your Model Number

You will find the model number on a sticker or rating plate. This can usually be found on an inner panel behind the salad drawer, or on an internal wall within the fridge.

Model numbers usually consist of a manner of letters and numbers, and may also include characters such as a slash (/) or a dash (-).
