      Washing Machine Carbon Brush - Pack of 2

      Your washing machine won't function properly if its carbon brushes are worn down and used up. Get it sorted with this set of carbon brushes from eSpares.

      Carbon brushes are in constant contact with the motor in your washer, so it's likely that it will eventually become worn out. With the help of this replacement carbon brush from eSpares, your washer will be back in full working order in no time!
