      Washing Machine Door Interlock

      If your washing machine is not starting, but everything seems to be working, the fault could be with the door interlock. If the door interlock fails, it means that your machine will not operate.

      There's no need to worry, this genuine spare part might just be what you're after to solve the problem and fix your washing machine to ensure it's always ready for action whenever you need it to be.
