Where to Find your Model Number

You will find the model number on a sticker or rating plate.

For cookers and ovens this can usually be found around the frame, behind the door of the appliance.

For hobs this can usually be found on the underside, side or back of the appliance.

Model numbers usually consist of a manner of letters and numbers, and may also include characters such as a slash (/) or a dash (-).
