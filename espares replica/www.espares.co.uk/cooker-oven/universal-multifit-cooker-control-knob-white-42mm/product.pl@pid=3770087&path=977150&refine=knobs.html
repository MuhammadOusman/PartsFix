      High Quality Replacement Multifit Cooker Control Knob - White

      If your cooker is in need of a replacement control knob, this compatible spare may just be what you are looking for! This universal control knob is white in colour and also comes with five different sized spindles to help you fit it to your cooker/hob. We have this replacement control knob that could solve your problem, and get you back to being able to have full control over your oven again. 

      Please note: This spare is a single control knob. Should you need a full set, you will need to order more than one control knob for your appliance.

      Specification:

      • 1 x Control Knob 5 x different sized spindles, (2 x 14mm, 2 x 28mm, 1 x 32mm)
      • Diameter: 42mm
      • Indicator pointer, (we do not have universal knobs with temperature markings)
      • PLEASE NOTE: This universal control knob will look cosmetically different to the original knob
      • For fitting guidance please watch the 'How To' video, as we do not have a list of appliances they are confirmed suitable for.
