      If the lamp in your oven has blown and your oven is now left in the dark, let eSpares help bring some light back to the situation. This universal branded bulb will fit most cookers that require an E14 SES 40W bulb.

      This high quality bulb has been manufactured to a temperature rating of 300°C, so it is safe to use in domestic ovens. With the help of eSpares and this replacement bulb, we can get you back to being able to easily see your meals in your oven.

      • Cap Fitting: E14/SES
      • Lumens: 400lm
